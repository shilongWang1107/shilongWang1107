## About Me

- My research focuses on **multimodal learning**, **cross-modal retrieval**, and **few-shot class-incremental learning**.
- I am interested in combining **large language models**, **vision-language models**, and **semantic priors** to improve representation learning.
- I enjoy turning research ideas into reproducible implementations and open-source projects.
